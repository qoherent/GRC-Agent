# Wiki account
To edit pages, you currently need a wiki account. 
## Requesting a Wiki Account
Since bots have made it infeasible, even after enabling mitigations, moving behind a bot-blocking CDN, and having captchas, to allow people to sign up through a web form, we ask would-be contributors to: 
  * Reach out to us via Matrix [Chat](https://wiki.gnuradio.org/index.php?title=Chat "Chat") (Discord bridge is fine, too) in the `#gnuradio:gnuradio.org` room, or the [discuss-gnuradio Mailing List](https://wiki.gnuradio.org/index.php?title=MailingLists "MailingLists"),
  * Tell us what you would like the account for (can be very short like "fix page on tubthumping"). If you want a wiki user name that's not the same as the name you contacted us with, tell us.
